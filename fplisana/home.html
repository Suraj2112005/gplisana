Welcome to Government Polytechnic Lisana

Located in a peaceful and green area of Rewari, Haryana. Our college provides a calm and friendly environment that helps students to focus on their education and personal growth.
We believe in offering quality technical education along with discipline and moral values. Our goal is not only to teach from books but also to build responsible and confident individuals.
Our experienced and helpful teachers are always ready to guide students. We also focus on sports, cultural activities, and skill development programs to ensure overall growth.
